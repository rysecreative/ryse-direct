Guesty, Hospitable (Professional plan or higher), Lodgify, Hostaway, Hostfully, OwnerRez, Smoobu, Tokeet, Uplisting, and iGMS. Don't see yours? Email us — we're always adding partners.
Q.02 What platform will my website be built on? +
Lite and DFY plans are built on WordPress. You get full admin access — your site, your platform, your control.
Q.03 Do I get to pick my website's design? +
Yes. You'll have five homepage designs to choose from. From there we tailor the entire site using your brand's colors, typography, and logo.
Q.04 How will guests be able to book my property? +
All bookings and payments are handled through your PMS's direct booking widget, embedded on your property pages. Calendar availability syncs automatically.
Q.05 If I go DFY, what do I need to provide? +
A detailed intake form: your Airbnb property links, iCal links, domain name, business name, logo, and branding info. We take it from there.
Q.06 Help — not sure which package to pick. +
If you want to learn how to add your own images and copy after we set up the site, go with Lite. If you want a hands-off, fully-built site delivered ready to go, choose DFY. Either way, you own the site.
Q.07 When will my site be live? +
After purchase and intake, expect a 1-week turnaround for Lite and 2 weeks for DFY — often sooner.
Q.08 Do you help with logo design? +
Not at the moment. We'll happily refer you to incredible freelancers who can deliver a great logo in 1–3 days.
